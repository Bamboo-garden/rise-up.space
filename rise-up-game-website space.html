About Rise Up

Rise Up is an incredibly addictive game where your reflexes and precision are put to the ultimate test. Your mission is simple yet challenging: protect a fragile balloon as it rises through a series of unpredictable obstacles.

Using your mouse or touchscreen, you must position a shield to deflect or avoid various objects that threaten to pop your balloon. As you progress, the obstacles become increasingly difficult, requiring quick thinking and precise movements.

With its simple concept but challenging gameplay, Rise Up has become a global sensation, captivating players of all ages. How high can you go? Play now and find out!

How to Play

1

Control the Shield

Use your mouse, trackpad, or finger (on mobile) to move the shield around your balloon.

2

Protect Your Balloon

Place the shield between your balloon and incoming obstacles to deflect them away.

3

Rise Higher

The higher your balloon rises, the more points you earn. Try to reach new heights with each game!
